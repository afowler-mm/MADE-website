MADE (Maine Ad + Design) is the oldest advertising club in the United States. For more than a century, it has championed creativity and professional growth for Maine’s designers, marketers, and creative professionals. Through the Broderson Awards and year-round programming, MADE supports agencies, freelancers, and students working in and for Maine.
